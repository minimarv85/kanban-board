## High-Potential Low-Cost Ideas (UK Market)

### 1. AI-Powered Services (HIGH POTENTIAL)
- **Automation for local businesses**: Roofers, plumbers, restaurants need booking/quote/follow-up automation
- **Chief of Staff as a Service**: Act as virtual COO for entrepreneurs (£2-5k/month retainer potential)
- Cost to start: Your time + AI tools (£0-100/mo)

### 2. Micro-SaaS / Digital Products
- **Industry-specific tools**: Find a frustration, build a £29-99/mo solution
- **Templates/swipe files**: For other businesses or creators
- **Prompt libraries / AI workflows**: Sell to businesses adopting AI
- Cost to start: £0-500 (hosting)

### 3. Professional Services
- **Copywriting**: £200-1000 per project, low overhead
- **Digital marketing**: SEO, content, email for local businesses
- **Bookkeeping**: £30-50/hour, recurring clients
- **CV writing/Career coaching**: £50-200 per client
- Cost to start: Laptop + internet (£0)

### 4. E-commerce Light
- **Dropshipping**: No inventory, but competitive
- **Print-on-demand**: T-shirts, merch, low risk
- **Curated subscriptions**: Niche products, recurring revenue
- Cost to start: £100-500

### 5. Local Service Automation
- **White-label operations**: Handle backend for existing businesses
- **Review management**: Businesses pay for 5-star review strategies
- **Social media management**: Content + posting for local businesses
- Cost to start: £0-200

## Key Trends for 2026
1. **AI adoption gap**: Most businesses want AI but don't know how to implement it
2. **Automation fatigue**: Businesses overwhelmed by tools, need simplified solutions
3. **Creator economy**: Tools, templates, workflows for content creators
4. **Local digital**: Physical businesses still need digital transformation
5. **Subscription fatigue**: People want fewer, better tools → integration services
